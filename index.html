About Us

History

Established in 2011, Vidhaigal NGO has dedicated the past 13+ years to fostering social harmony and environmental sustainability. Through collaborative efforts with various stakeholders, the organization has implemented impactful initiatives including tree plantations, environmental awareness campaigns, and educational support programs for underprivileged children. Vidhaigal continues to inspire and lead by example, striving to create a balanced and prosperous future for all.

Read More

Mission

To build a healthy society for tomorrow with active participation from students, promote environmental conservation, and enhance equitable food security and a prosperous community through the power and energy of youth.

Read More

Vision

To create a green and sustainable society, nurture a community enriched by education and knowledge, and advance through core principles of forest growth, environmental protection, and overall progress.

Read More

Goal

Vidhaigal NGO envisions a sustainable and progressive future through a well-structured roadmap that addresses the pressing needs of society and the environment. Guided by our core values and years of impactful service, our future plans focus on amplifying our efforts and driving meaningful change across key areas.

  • Environmental Sustainability
  • Educational Empowerment
  • Community Development
  • Social Awareness and Advocacy
  • Youth and Volunteer Engagement

Our Projects

Ambakkam MiniForest

Vidhaigal NGO, partnering with the BOSCH India Foundation, is leading a 4-acre tree plantation project in Ambakkam, Kanchipuram, focusing on 21 endangered native species to enhance biodiversity.

Read More

Deveriyambakkam Community Forest

In partnership with Sangalpataru Foundation, Vidhaigal NGO has successfully established a 15-acre forest on government land in Devariyambakkam, Kanchipuram. We planted a diverse range of trees, including banyan, peepal, iluppa, fig, guava, and mango, to support biodiversity and enhance rainfall.

Read More

Thirumukkodal EcoForest

We are planting native trees like Kongan neem, Aalam, Arasu, and more on government public land in Thirumukkodal village, Salavakkam Union, Kanchipuram district, with necessary permissions from the district administration.
